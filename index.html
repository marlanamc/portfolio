About Me

I build products that help adults learn, and I care about making them simple and accessible.

I came to software through data and teaching. I earned an MS in Economics from Georgia Tech, where my thesis used panel regression on NCES data to study institutional factors linked to non-completion at for-profit colleges and tuition trends in higher education. I then worked as a data analyst creating Tableau dashboards and running forecasting models. As a Research Analyst at Harvard Kennedy School, I supported statistical research using R and STATA. This trained me to be precise with data and to ship work people can trust.
