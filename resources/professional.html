About These Resources

As someone who personally managed 100+ job applications during a career transition, I understand how overwhelming the job search can be. These resources are designed to help you stay organized, motivated, and strategic throughout your job search journey.

From tracking applications to preparing for interviews, these tools will help you approach your job search with confidence and structure. Many of these resources I created for myself and refined through real-world use.
